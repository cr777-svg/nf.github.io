Healthy Foods to Gain Weight Fast

1. Milk & Banana

A blend of milk, bananas, nuts, honey can be a great way to gain weight.

2. Coffee & Cream

Add cream to your coffee. This is a simple way to add in more calories.

3. Rice 

A cup of rice contains about 200 calories and it is also good source of carbohydrates, which contribute to weight gain. 

4. Cheese

Cheese is a very good source of protein and rich in healthy fats.Add it to meals if you need a calorie and flavor boost.

5. Whole-grain bread

This bread contains complex carbohydrates, which can promote weight gain. Some also contain seeds, which provide added benifits.   

6. Pasta

Pasta can provide a calorically dense and carbohydrate-rich path to healthy weight gain.

7. Fish

Salmon is rich in healthy fats, making it a good choice for those looking to gain weight. 

8. Egg 

Eggs are a good Source of protein, healthy fat, and other nutrients. Most nutrients are contained in the yolk.
