ANTIOXIDANT DRINKS

1. Tomato Juice

Tomato Juice is a concentrated source of powerful antioxidant like lycopene, a plant pigment that has been linked to impressive health benefits.In fact,Americans a get more than 80% of their lycopene from tomatoes and products like tomato juice. Lycopene protect your cells from free radical damage,thereby reducing inflammation in the body.1 Cup Tomato juice Daily (32 mg  LYCOPENE) had significant reduction of inflammatory proteins.Its  increases blood level  and reduction in cholesterol and waist Circumference.Also, its contain Vitamin C and beta-carotene.


2. Green Tea

Green tea is higher in protective polyphenols. The  major polyphenols in green tea are flavonoids, the most active of which are catechins and epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG),which function as powerful antioxidants.The antioxidant effects of  polyphenolsappears to be greater than those of vitamin C.

3. Red Wine 

Compared to green grapes, red grapes contain more antioxidents, which are found in the skin of dark red and purple grapes used to make red wine.They Contain resveratrol  and group of antioxidants called anthocyanins,which are linked to controlling blood pressure, reducing damage to blood vessels and protecting against heart disease.
 

4. Acai Berry Juice 

Acai berries have an incredibly high amount of antioxidants edging out other antioxidant rich fruits like blueberries and cranberries. The antioxidant content of food is typically measured by an oxygen Radical Absorbance Capacity or ORAC score.In the case of acai 100 gm of frozen pulp has an ORAC of 15,405, whereas the same amount of blueberries has score 4,669. That's quite a difference! 

5. Blueberry Juice 

Blueberries Contain Vitamin C, K , Manganese , some fibers and nutrients.The main antioxidant compounds in blueberries belong to a family of polyphenols antioxidants called flavonoids. Reduce DNA damage,which may help protect against aging and cancer.its may help prevent heart disease.its helpful for reduce muscle damage after strenuous exercise. 

6. Grape Juice

Grape juice is great alternative for those who don't drink wine, because its same healthy flavonoids that its alcoholic counterpart. Grape juice has plenty of other health benefits , too, including reducing the risk of blood clots and heart disease. One downside is that most great juice does contain lot of sugar, but drinking the all natural stuff in moderation is the key getting the health benefits without the downsides.When picking a grape juice always go for the purple colored juices, and try to avoid anything with added sugars, colors or flavoring.   

7. Coffee

Coffee beans carry the lots of the absolute highest level of antioxidants of any other staple in diet.That includes the much touted super foods like kale, dark chocolate, blueberries and acai berries.Coffee is very rich antioxidants including polyphenols and hydrocinnamic acids that improve health and reduce your risk of  several diseases.

8. Orange Juice

Orange juice contain Vitamin C, Folate, Potassium, Magnesium and some nutrients.its a good source of antioxidants like flavonoids, carotenoids and ascorbic acid.Help to prevent kidney stones, improve heart health and decrease inflammation.

9. Cranberry Juice 

Chemical called free radicals accumulate in the body as you age. These free radicals cause oxidative damage. There is a link between oxidative damage and health issues include cancer, diabetes heart disease and digestive health.Some of the chemicals in cranberry juice are antioxidants that fight harmful free radicals.There presence also means that it can help fight age related damage to your body tissues.

10. Coconut Water

Coconut contains antioxidants that help to combat free radicals.its also an excellent source of several other important nutrients like zinc, copper, calcium, and iron.One cup of coconut water a day will provide you with over 15% of your daily recommended intake of magnesium, manganese, and potassium.One downside is that despite all of vitamins and minerals.it can be high in sodium,so you drink it in moderation.it can also lower your risk for cancer and other serious conditions.
